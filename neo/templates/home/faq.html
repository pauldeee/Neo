Frequently Asked Questions

How does Neo work?

Neo works by taking accounts you already own and allowing you to interact with them in one location.

Why would I want to use Neo?

Neo is much more than just one location for your trading-financials. Neo can place trades, help you trade and even trade for you.

What is Neo's Next-day Up?

Next-day Up is the predictive strategy that Neo uses when you enable automated trading.

How it works:

Neo looks at thousands of stocks and predicts if the next day the stock will close higher or lower than today's close.

Next-day Up then analyzes its predicitons against past data, keeping only the top 10 trades with the probability of success.

Risk Handling

Neo follows a 1% /10% rule. This means that on any given trade Next-day Up will never trade more than 1% of your total portfolio value on a single trade -- never risking more than 10% of total funds on a single set of trades
