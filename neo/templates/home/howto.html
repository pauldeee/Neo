How To

Connect an Account

After registering for Neo, simply hover over the "My Account" menu and select "External Accounts." On this page you can select an institution you'd like to add, provide the required information and click "Connect Account!"

Placing a Manual Trade

Is there a stock you really like? You can manually trade it by searching for the stock name or ticker in the search bar along the top of the webpage. After searching, you'll be shown the asset and can buy or sell. If you want to buy based on dollar amount select "Dollars", if you want to buy based on number of shares select "shares" then click the "Place BUY Order" button. These same steps can be used for selling. Simply use the "Amount to SELL" field and the "Place SELL Order" button

Enabling Fully Automated Predictive Trading

After creating your Neo account and connect an external account, simply navigate to "My Account > Trading." Here you will find the Trading Center. Here you will find Neo's "Market-Wide Predictive Trading bot where you can enable Neo's Next Day Up strategy. Simply click "START" to start trading automatically or "STOP" to stop automatic trading with Next-Day-Up.
